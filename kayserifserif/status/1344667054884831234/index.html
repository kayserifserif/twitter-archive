Graham, 60, East London and South Yorkshire:

"cor blimey", sometimes as "gorblimey", minced curse for "God blind me" ("Cor blimey. Right on me nut.")

"ta", "thanks" in Northern England and Wales, from child's pronunciation of "thank you" ("I'll give it a miss, ta.")
