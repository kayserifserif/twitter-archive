menu (n): 1837 from French menu de repas "list of what is served at a meal", French menu (adj) "small, detailed". computer sense: 1967 from "any detailed list".

菜單 (cài dān): 菜=dish 單=list. expanded to computer sense: 開始菜單 ("start dish list"), 應用菜單 ("app dish list")
